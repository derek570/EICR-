# Data Processing Agreement

## EICR-oMatic 3000 - Data Protection Terms

**Effective Date:** Upon acceptance

---

## 1. Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the terms of use for EICR-oMatic 3000 ("the System"). By using this System, you agree to process personal data in accordance with this agreement and applicable data protection laws, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## 2. Definitions

- **Personal Data**: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, including names, addresses, contact details, and property information.
- **Data Controller**: The user of this System who determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.
- **Data Processor**: EICR-oMatic 3000 system and its operators.
- **Data Subject**: The individual whose personal data is being processed (e.g., property owners, tenants).

## 3. Data Processing Purposes

The System processes personal data solely for the following purposes:

- Generating Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICR)
- Generating Electrical Installation Certificates (EIC)
- Managing inspection records and job workflows
- Creating PDF certificates and documentation
- Maintaining audit trails for compliance purposes

## 4. Categories of Personal Data

The following categories of personal data may be processed:

- Client names and contact information
- Property addresses
- Installation details and condition observations
- Inspector information and signatures
- Dates and times of inspections

## 5. Your Responsibilities as Data Controller

By using this System, you confirm that:

1. **Lawful Basis**: You have a lawful basis for processing the personal data entered into the System (typically contractual necessity or legitimate interests).

2. **Data Subject Rights**: You will respond to any data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, etc.) within the statutory timeframes.

3. **Data Accuracy**: You will ensure that personal data entered is accurate and kept up to date.

4. **Data Minimisation**: You will only enter personal data that is necessary for the stated purposes.

5. **Security**: You will keep your login credentials secure and not share them with unauthorised persons.

6. **Notification**: You will notify data subjects about the processing of their data as required by law.

## 6. Data Security Measures

The System implements the following security measures:

- Password-protected access with bcrypt hashing
- Account lockout after failed login attempts
- Session timeout after 24 hours of inactivity
- Audit logging of all data access and modifications
- Data isolation between users

## 7. Data Retention

- Active job data is retained while in use
- Completed certificates are retained for compliance purposes
- You may request deletion of your data at any time (GDPR Article 17)
- Audit logs are retained for regulatory compliance

## 8. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach affecting data processed in the System:

- We will notify you without undue delay
- You are responsible for notifying the ICO and affected data subjects as required by law
- You must report any suspected breaches to the system administrator immediately

## 9. International Transfers

Personal data processed in this System is stored and processed within the United Kingdom. No international transfers occur without explicit consent.

## 10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access personal data held about you
- Rectify inaccurate personal data
- Erase personal data (right to be forgotten)
- Restrict processing in certain circumstances
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

## 11. Contact Information

For any data protection queries or to exercise your rights, please contact the system administrator.

## 12. Acceptance

By clicking "I have read and agree to the Data Processing Agreement" and continuing to use the System, you:

1. Confirm you have read and understood this DPA
2. Agree to process personal data in accordance with these terms
3. Acknowledge your responsibilities as a Data Controller
4. Accept the security measures implemented by the System
