## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES

### 2.1 What CertMate Does

CertMate is an AI-assisted productivity tool designed to help qualified UK electricians create Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICRs), Electrical Installation Certificates (EICs), and related electrical certification documents. The Services include:

(a) **Voice Recording and Transcription** -- The App captures audio via the device microphone during inspection and testing. Audio is streamed in real time to Deepgram, a third-party speech-to-text service, for transcription;

(b) **AI-Assisted Data Extraction** -- Transcripts and contextual data are processed by Anthropic's Claude Sonnet, a third-party AI service, to extract structured certificate data (addresses, readings, observations, circuit details);

(c) **CCU Photo Analysis** -- Photographs of consumer units are analysed by OpenAI's GPT Vision, a third-party AI service, to identify circuit breakers, RCD types, and board configurations;

(d) **Certificate Generation** -- The App generates PDF certificates using data captured through the above methods and manual user input;

(e) **Local and Cloud Data Storage** -- Certificate data is stored locally on your device and may be synchronised to our cloud infrastructure for backup and multi-device access.

### 2.2 What CertMate Does NOT Do

The Services are a productivity aid only. CertMate does NOT:

(a) Verify, validate, certify, or guarantee the accuracy of any certificate, data, reading, observation, or recommendation;

(b) Inspect, test, or assess any electrical installation;

(c) Replace, substitute, or supplement the professional judgement, expertise, or competence of a qualified electrician;

(d) Guarantee compliance with BS 7671, the IET Wiring Regulations, the Electricity at Work Regulations 1989, or any other applicable law, standard, regulation, code of practice, or guidance;

(e) Verify your professional qualifications, competence, registration, or insurance status;

(f) Create any professional, contractual, or fiduciary relationship between CertMate and any third party to whom you issue a certificate;

(g) Provide legal, regulatory, or professional advice of any kind.

### 2.3 No Endorsement

CertMate is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or approved by the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), the British Standards Institution (BSI), the National Inspection Council for Electrical Installation Contracting (NICEIC), the Electrical Contractors' Association (ECA), NAPIT, or any other professional, regulatory, or certification body.
