(a) Process your data only in accordance with our documented instructions;

(b) **Not use your data to train, improve, or develop their AI models**;

(c) **Not retain your data beyond the time necessary to complete processing**;

(d) Implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures;

(e) Not sub-process your data without our prior written consent;

(f) Assist us in fulfilling our obligations regarding your data rights.

Deepgram processes audio in real time and, under our agreement, operates with zero data retention after transcription is complete. Deepgram is SOC 2 Type 2 certified.
